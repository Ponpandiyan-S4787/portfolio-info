Project details : - ( Airline )

Booking flow
  • The booking process in the Airline Management System is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, allowing customers to easily search for and secure their desired flights. Users can input their travel details, such as departure and arrival locations, travel dates, and passenger information
  • The system then displays available flights, including options for direct and connecting routes, along with seat availability
Re-booking flow
  • Re-booking is equally straightforward. If customers need to change their travel plans, they can access their booking history through their user profiles.
  • Users are notified of any additional fees or credits before finalizing the re-booking. With intuitive navigation and clear prompts, the Airline Management System ensures a seamless experience for both booking and re-booking, catering to the evolving needs of travelers.
Baggage info
  • The Airline Management System provides a clear baggage policy tailored to enhance the travel experience. Each fare class-economy, premium, and business has specific limits for checked and carry-on luggage, including weight and size restrictions.
  • During booking, customers can indicate their baggage needs and view any additional fees for extra luggage. After confirming their bookings, users can manage baggage preferences through their profiles, allowing for modifications as needed.
